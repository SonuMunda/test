Swimming can be booked 6 days in advance from 8am via the App or on the website under members. You can also book a swim slot in person at your home club.

If you are looking to freeze your membership this can be done online. If you joined after July 2020 you are able to do this in the App. If you joined before July 2020 you can do this on the website under members. Please action freezes before the 21st of the previous month.

Members may request to freeze their Membership from a minimum of one month to a maximum of three consecutive calendar months, once per membership year. Memberships can only be frozen for full calendar months. A fee of rupees 500 per month shall be applied to any freeze granted. Any months frozen will be added to the end of the current membership term.
